How to Use

1. Choose a Template

Select from 5 professionally designed templates. Each has a unique style suited for different types of content.

2. Select Format

Pick between Square (Instagram feed), Portrait (4:5), or Story (9:16) formats depending on where you'll post.

3. Add Your Content

  • Headline: The main text that grabs attention
  • Tagline: Supporting text that adds context
  • Brand Name: Your company name for attribution
  • Image: Upload a photo or use the placeholder

4. Customize Colors

Pick from preset colors or choose your own brand color using the color picker.

5. Download

Click download to get your high-resolution image ready for social media!
